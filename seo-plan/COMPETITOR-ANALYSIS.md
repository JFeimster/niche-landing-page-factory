# Competitor Analysis (Planning Baseline)

Project: `distilledfunding.com`  
Date: 2026-03-27  
Note: this is a strategic baseline for implementation planning; validate final competitor set with live SERP pulls before execution.

## 1) Competitor Types to Track

- Direct funding marketplaces and lenders targeting SMB cashflow gaps.
- Vertical-specific financing publishers (contractor, trucking, restaurant, healthcare niches).
- High-authority financial education sites ranking informational intents.
- AI answer surfaces and aggregator snippets that can intercept top-of-funnel queries.

## 2) Initial Domain Watchlist (Working Set)

- National funding marketplaces (general SMB financing comparisons)
- Contractor trade finance publishers (HVAC/plumbing/roofing intent)
- Gig economy finance content players
- E-commerce seller funding comparison sites
- Local-business financing advisors with strong local SEO footprints

## 3) Gap Map vs Likely Competitors

Expected competitor strengths:
- Deep informational libraries and glossary coverage
- Strong backlink profiles
- Established domain trust and branded searches

Current opportunity for Moonshine:
- Faster vertical page deployment velocity
- Better conversion UX on high-intent landing pages
- More direct "decision path" content (Apply vs Prep vs No)
- Better cross-linking between vertical hubs and actionable guides

## 4) Keyword Gap Themes to Prioritize

- "fast funding for [vertical]"
- "[vertical] financing without tax returns"
- "[vertical] cash flow gap funding"
- "deposit based funding [vertical]"
- "[vertical] net 30 / net 60 project cashflow funding"
- "MCA alternatives for [vertical]"

## 5) Content and SERP Feature Strategy

- Win featured snippets with direct-answer intros and list formatting.
- Add FAQ clusters to capture PAA intent.
- Build comparison pages for high CPC terms:
  - RBF vs MCA
  - LOC vs Advance
  - "fast funding" vs "lower-cost funding"

## 6) Competitive Monitoring Cadence

Weekly:
- Track top 20 target terms for active verticals.
- Capture ranking movement and SERP feature presence.

Monthly:
- Re-score competitor page depth for each active vertical.
- Update internal link map and publish net-new gap content.

Quarterly:
- Refresh target keyword universe and retire low-opportunity topics.
- Re-prioritize vertical rollout waves using conversion + ranking data.

## 7) Success Benchmarks

- Month 3: outrank weaker long-tail pages for at least 5 target terms.
- Month 6: hold top-10 positions for core long-tail terms in 3+ verticals.
- Month 12: establish authority cluster coverage in 10+ verticals with stable top-10 visibility.
